### 3. Basic Solution Methods

- **Monte Carlo Methods**: Learning from complete episodes with model-free prediction and control.

- **Q-Learning and SARSA**: Introduction to off-policy and on-policy control solutions.

- **Scaling and Optimization**: Techniques for scaling up RL solutions, including parallelization and distributed computing.

- **Ethics and Safety in RL**: Considerations for ethical deployment and ensuring safety in RL applications.
